Our Mission

Built for Business Owners Who Build

Tomar Media was founded on a simple premise: the business owners constructing our cities deserve a publication that speaks directly to their world. Not a general business magazine that occasionally covers diversity. A real digital magazine — live feeds, real stories, real contracts.

Every day we aggregate the most relevant news across procurement, policy, certification, funding and project awards from dozens of vetted sources — federal agencies, trade publications, state government, advocacy organizations and national media — delivered in one fast, mobile-ready interface.

Whether you're a certified WBE architect, an MBE contractor bidding on NYC DOT work, a DBE firm tracking federal infrastructure spending, or a VOB pursuing VA contracts — Tomar Media is your daily briefing.

Why Digital

Built for the
Speed of
Business

Traditional print operates on weekly or monthly cycles. Contracts, grants and policy changes move daily. Tomar Media is built on live RSS feeds and real-time aggregation so you always know what's happening now.

01

Real-Time News Aggregation

Every tab pulls from live RSS feeds across 50+ vetted sources — federal agencies, trade publications, advocacy organizations and national business media. Articles refresh every visit, current to the hour.

02

Category-Specific Editorial Feeds

Unlike general aggregators, every category is purpose-built. WBE feeds only surface women-owned business news. The NY/NJ AEC tab exclusively covers New York and New Jersey construction procurement. Each community gets its own dedicated stream.

03

Magazine-Quality Presentation

News doesn't have to look like a spreadsheet. Tomar Media presents live RSS content in a full digital magazine format — hero stories, photo-driven layouts, curated sidebars and a real-time ticker.

04

Mobile-First for Busy Owners

Business owners in AEC are on job sites, in meetings, on the move. Every layout and navigation decision is made with mobile reading in mind — fast-loading, scrollable, tap-friendly.

05

Referenced Against Leading Digital Media

Tomar Media's standards draw from the best in digital news: the bold typography of The Atlantic, the live-feed efficiency of Axios, the procurement focus of Government Executive, and the community voice of Black Enterprise — combined for diverse AEC business owners.
